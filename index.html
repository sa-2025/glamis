Glamis

Introducing The Desert Glamis

Have you ever heard of the desert called Glamis? It's really cool. People take their atvs and dirt bikes and ride the soft sand dunes. Theres many famous spots out there. Some of them are hidden and are really hard to find. But not impossible. People go out there on holidays like New Years, Halloween, Presidents weekened and many more. The most popular holiday to go visit Glamis is New Years.

Brands

Theres so many different brands and types of vehicles you can buy. Some are can-ams, razors, sandrails, quads, two wheelers, three wheelers and many more. Glamis is owned by one of these brands called Polaris, Polaris has ownedthe desert since 2018. Many events are held by big brands. In january Genesis Supreme does an event called the Dune Cleanup Project. All you have to do is drive around the dunes and pick up all the trash you see. Every bag you turn in you get a ticket and at 5pm a giant raffle will begin. Genesis Supreme gives out tools, parts, electronics and many more cool items.
